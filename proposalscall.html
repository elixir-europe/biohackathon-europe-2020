The call for hacking projects is open from 2nd March and will close on 2th April 2019. The project leader will submit through the EasyChair submission

EasyChair submission

Information required

Accommodation is available in single rooms only.

  • Title and description of the project (300 word max)
  • Representation: which will include details around alignment with ELIXIR platforms/communities or project’s external community importance
  • Project Lead’s contact details who will be main correspondent (we will only contact this main correspondent even though there are multiple leads)
  • Expected outcomes from BioHackathon and beyond
  • Expected Audience: type of participant needed to help with the project
  • Number of days for the hack (NB it is expected that all participants stay for the 4 days even though some projects may be shorter)
  • Is there a specific person (2 max) from Europe you would like to invite that is critical to the success of the project? (name /institute/email)

Selection criteria

The programme organising committee will select the project based on the following criteria

  • Scientific interest/alignment to ELIXIR platforms/community goals
  • Are outcomes achievable in the timeframe suggested
  • How many nodes/people likely to participate
