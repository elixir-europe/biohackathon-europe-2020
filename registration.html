  • General registration will open in June (limited to 170 participants)

Going virtual

After much discussion internally at the Hub and with feedback from the programme committee, we have decided to run this year BioHackathon virtually instead of Face to Face.

We realise this will be a disappointment for some of you, but after analysing the possible risk around local closures or travel bans due to the covid-19 pandemic, going virtual was the best option.

We think this will allow everyone to participate in the BioHackathon and deliver tangible outcomes from the selected projects.

Please register ASAP (before 18th Sept), as space will be limited and participation during all 4 days is expected

Code of Conduct

The Biohackathon2020 will adhere to the ELIXIR code of Conduct and there will be ELIXIR Allies present at the event to ensure adoption of the Code of Conduct.
